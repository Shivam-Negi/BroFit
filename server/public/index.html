About Brofit

Brofit is a revolutionary gym membership app that offers cutting-edge features to gym owners and members alike. Whether you're a gym owner looking to digitize your attendance and member management, or a fitness enthusiast wanting to track your progress and access workout tutorials, Brofit has got you covered.

Features

  • Real-time attendance tracking based on location
  • Live graph showing current members and hourly member count
  • Easy member registration and profile management for gym owners
  • Member access to attendance records in a calendar view
  • Fitness timer app and workout tutorials
  • Built-in chat app for all gym members
